My expertise is in fish stock assessments, animal population dynamics, and biological statistics. I was trained for my Ph.D. in an interdisciplinary program, Quantitative Ecology & Resource Management (QERM) and for my MS in fisheries at the University of Washington (Seattle, WA). Now I work as Professor in the College of Fisheries Sciences at Pukyong National University, Korea. My previous positions include tenure track Assistant Professor at the University of Massachusetts-Dartmouth (Dartmouth, MA), and Fishery Quantitative Scientist at Columbia River Inter-Tribal Fish Commission (Portland, OR).
